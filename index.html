Projects

LI-FI

Light Fidelity (Li-Fi) is a very new concept of communication which delivers data like text, audio, video at high speeds using LEDs to transmit data using only light brightness. The spectrum which is used unlike Wi-fi is of the visible region. In this method, the LED is used to transmit data across the visible light spectrum. This Technology when used as an alternative to wifi offers incredible performance, efficiency, security with low latency etc. This communication is achieved by blinking the light at high rate than what is visible to human eye.

FARMOSMART

This system is based on the concept of In-House farming with the help of artificial lighting and use of various chemical substances that will result in crop production at a faster and efficient rate as compared to conventional farming techniques. This process helps in reducing required crop growth time and also makes farming mobile. There are a wide variety of applications of this system. For eg: This system can grow fresh herbs within a small period of time which in turn can help in serving better quality food at a variety of restaurants across the globe.

SMART DUSTBIN

Lhe smart dustbin project is an IoT based waste management system. It senses the level of trash in a dustbin using an ultrasonic sensor attached to the dustbin cover. When the level reaches above 80%, an app displays the real time data about the occupancy status of the dustbin in the form of percentage. The app also shows the location of the dustbins placed across the campus. This system helps the management people work easily & more efficiently round the clock on disposal of waste materials from the dustbins. The project has been approved to be implemented in the campus.
